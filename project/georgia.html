🇬🇪 Georgia

About

Georgia is a transcontinental country at the intersection of Eastern Europe and Western Asia in the Caucasus region, bordered by the Black Sea, Russia, Turkey, Armenia, and Azerbaijan. Its capital is Tbilisi, and it is known for its ancient history, 8,000-year wine tradition, diverse landscapes from Caucasus mountains to Black Sea beaches, and medieval architecture.
